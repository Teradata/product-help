Access your projects and monitor recent activity.

## What the page shows


-   **Recent Projects**

    Shows projects you recently created or updated, including Git connection status. Select **View all** to see all projects in the **Projects** page.

    A Project consists of models, datasets, and other resources you use to train, evaluate and deploy a model in your environment.

    -   In the **Projects** page, in the row of a project, you can select ![Kebab menu.](Images/kbt1547502809538.png) > **View Git details** to check for connectivity errors.


-   **Recent jobs**

    Shows the most recent jobs that ran for models in your projects.


-   **Settings**

    In the upper-right corner of the ModelOps dashboard, select **Your account** ![Person icon](Images/mci1652327190262.svg) > **Account settings** or **Session details** to access ModelOps [account settings](jue1725407850414.md) or [session details](xfm1725407885906.md), respectively.
