**SCIM for Automated User Provisioning**

SCIM (System for Cross-domain Identity Management) enables automated user lifecycle management between your organization's Identity provider and Teradata. It keeps user accounts and group memberships in sync between your Identity provider and Teradata.

When you configure SCIM, adding a user to the right group in your Identity provider automatically creates their Teradata account with the correct role already applied. Removing them from the group revokes their access immediately.

**SCIM capabilities**

-   New users are automatically granted Teradata access the moment they are assigned in your Identity provider.


-   Access is revoked immediately when a user is removed or deactivated in your Identity provider.


-   Changes to user attributes (name, email, role) propagate automatically.


-   Map IdP groups to Teradata roles for scalable access control.


**Prerequisites**

Before enabling SCIM, confirm the following are in place:

-   Organization Admin role to access VantageCloud Enterprise Console.


-   Application administrator access to create SCIM integrations in your Identity provider.


-   Your Identity provider must be able to reach the Teradata SCIM endpoint over HTTPS (port 443).


-   Create a dedicated SCIM admin service account (username and password) before setup begins. Your IDP uses this account to authenticate to Teradata's SCIM endpoint.


**Enable sites**

1.  To enable sites, click **Enable** on the site that you want to enable.


1.  Enter your dedicated database account username and password that you created before starting the setup.


1.  Click **Next**.


1.  Use the SCIM client URL to configure users and groups from your identity provider.


1.  Click **Create client credentials**.


1.  Copy and add the Client ID and Client secret to your identity provider.


1.  Click **generate access token**.


1.  Copy the access token and use it to authorize.


1.  Test the connection configuration.


1.  Click **finish**.


1.  Check the status of the site.


1.  To edit or delete existing sites, select the menu (kabob icon) and click either **Edit** or **Delete**.


**Note**: Stopping a site stops automatic provisioning and syncing. If you restart the site, you must reconfigure automatic user provisioning.
