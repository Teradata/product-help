Review previous backup jobs that have run successfully or failed from the **History** tab.

Use the **Search** to select a specific backup job and review its history.

Select ![Kabob](Images/kws1630322576230.png) > **Details** to display detailed information about the backup job such as overview, objects, schedule, history, retained copies, and settings.

To view the run details on the select job such as errors, warning, and skipped objects on a backup, select the **History** tab > ![Kabob](Images/kws1630322576230.png) > **Details**. Skipped objects can be excluded from future runs of the job.
