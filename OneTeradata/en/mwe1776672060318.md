The metrics in AgentOps show the details of traces and latency.

Trace: Represents a complete execution path for a single interaction with an LLM or agent.

Span: Represents an individual operation within a trace.

Metric: Represents quantitative measurements used to assess model performance.

Optimization: Represents the process of refining prompts and configurations.

Evaluation: Represents the process of testing models and prompts against datasets.
