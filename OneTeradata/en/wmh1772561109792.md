A conversation with Tera typically follows this flow:

1.  **Initial request**

    Describe your goal and include the initial context and instructions.

    Example: Help me explore customer churn trends in our banking dataset.


1.  **Clarification, if needed**

    Tera may ask for more detail to better understand your request or confirm available access.

    Example: Which database should I search?


1.  **Response or options**

    Tera returns results, insights, or next-step options based on your request.

    Example: I found three tables related to customer activity. Would you like to explore their schemas?


1.  **Refinement**

    Use follow-up prompts to narrow the results or continue the analysis.

    Example: Show me the schema for the largest table and summarize the key columns.


1.  **Deeper exploration**

    Continue the conversation to review additional datasets, compare findings, or generate visualizations.


See [Structure your conversation](gxa1772561021277.md) on how to structure your conversation.
