Welcome to AI Studio, a single stop experience to meet your data and workload needs.

The homepage is your access to the AI Studio experience. From here, you can navigate to the AI Toolkit, learning resources, personal account settings, and interact with Terry, Teradata's AI-powered chat.

Use the left menu to view or move between the features in AI Studio. No matter what task you are performing, the panel is always accessible to take you back to the homepage. You can keep the panel minimized with just the icons showing or select (menu icon) to keep it expanded.

- [AI-assistant](undefined) (icon)
- Ask Terry system information, data analysis questions, and  questions about your data. Additional <chatbot> menu options include options to search chats and view recent chats.
- [Notebooks](undefined) (icon) Seamlessly collaborate with other members of your team by developing models, workflows, and data charts.
- [Vector store](undefined) (icon) Store and manage your data as vector embeddings for search integration and RAG applications.
- [ModelOps](undefined) (icon) Create, manage, and run models and put your use cases.
- [Integrations](undefined) (icon and link) Integrate third-party applications with AI Studio to manage projects and train models with ModelOps.
