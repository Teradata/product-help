A span represents an individual operation within a trace. Spans provide a granular view of system execution by decomposing a trace into smaller, measurable components. This structure enables visibility into both high-level workflows and detailed execution steps.

Key Characteristics of Spans

• Hierarchical structure with parent-child relationships.

• Represents a specific operation or task.

• Includes precise timing information.

• Maintains execution context across nested operations.

• Supports custom attributes for additional metadata.
