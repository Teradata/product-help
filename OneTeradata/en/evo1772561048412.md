The account settings page allows you to set your display preferences. These are personal settings that do not affect other users in the organization.

|Setting|Option|
|--------|-------|
|Your information|Username|
|Appearance|Light mode|
|Cookie preferences|Enable (default) or disable|
|Language|English, Spanish, and so on|
|Time and locale|Timezone, date format, and time format|

You can access the account settings from the toolbar.
