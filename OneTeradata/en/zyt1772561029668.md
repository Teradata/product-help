Chunking is the process of breaking down large documents or data into smaller, manageable, and semantically meaningful pieces called **chunks**.

Select either **Basic** or **Unstructured.io**.

**Basic**: Simple, fixed-size or recursive splitting methods.

**Unstructured.io**: Select if your documents are unstructured and multi-modal data, such as, text, PDFs, and images.

By default, each chunk contains 512 characters. If required, you can change this chunk size.

While changing the chunk size, you can also select header and/or footer or up to 1000 pixels.

**Note**: Selecting header and footer adds the header and footer to the data being processed.
