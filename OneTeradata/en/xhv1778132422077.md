When you open Teradata Native Notebooks for the first time, you must select an environment to run your notebook code.

1.  Select a node pool.

    1.  General compute.

        5 nodes of 16 vCPU, memory: 64 GB RAM


    1.  High memory.

        3 nodes of 64 vCPU, memory: 512 GB RAM


    1.  GPU accelerated.

        1 node of 8xNVIDIA H200, 64 CPU, memory: 503 GB RAM


1.  Configure resources for regular or GPU nodes.

    1.  [Configure Resources for Regular Nodes](vzi1778132810791.md)


    1.  [Configure Resources for GPU Nodes](axj1778133448257.md)
