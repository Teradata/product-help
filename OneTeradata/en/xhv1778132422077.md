Node pool refers to a shared group of servers. Each pool has different specifications. Select the pool that fits your workload.

1.  Select a node pool.

    1.  General compute: Use for SQL queries, exploratory analysis in Python, and in-database functions.

        5 nodes of 16 vCPU, memory: 64 GB RAM


    1.  High memory: Use when your workload loads large datasets fully into memory: multi-gigabyte DataFrames, or feature matrices that exhaust General compute.

        3 nodes of 64 vCPU, memory: 512 GB RAM
