Tune how a model responds based on your use case. Start with default values and adjust gradually to observe the effect on output quality.

## Parameters


-   **Temperature**

    Controls response variance and creativity.

    **Guidance**: Lower = predictable, factual. Higher = creative. Adjust in small steps (for example, ±0.1).


-   **Top-p**

    Limits the set of words the model considers when generating a response.

    **Guidance**: Lower = most likely words only. Higher = more variation. Adjust when temperature alone does not produce the desired effect.


-   **Frequency penalty**

    Reduces repeated words or phrases in a response.

    **Guidance**: Increase to reduce repeated terms. Keep low for technical or structured content.


-   **Presence penalty**

    Encourages the model to introduce new topics or ideas.

    **Guidance**: Increase to expand topics. Keep low for focused, single-topic responses.
