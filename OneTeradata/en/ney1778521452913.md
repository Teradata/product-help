Manage secure, governed access to AI models without exposing provider credentials. Virtual keys define who can access specific models and what limits apply, giving you centralized access control across teams and applications.

## What the page shows


-   **Key ID**

    Shows the unique identifier for the virtual key.


-   **Status**

    Shows whether the key is active or expired.


-   **Virtual key alias**

    Shows the descriptive name assigned to the key.


-   **Associated tags**

    Shows tags used to organize keys by team, department, or use case.


-   **User email ID**

    Shows the email address of the user assigned to the key.


-   **Created by**

    Shows who created the key.


-   **Created at**

    Shows the date and time the key was created.


Use **Search** to filter keys by name, tag, or user.

## Who does what


-   **Administrator**

    View all virtual keys. Create, manage, and delete keys. Assign keys to users.


-   **User (virtual key)**

    View only keys assigned to you. Use the key to access assigned models.
