You can choose either Basic or Unstructured.io for your vector collection.

**Basic**: This is the most efficient chunking method. Select Basic if you're working with straightforward data like contracts or policy documents.

**Note**: You may have split sentences or structure loss in complex documents and thus would affect the accuracy of the results. You can fix this by using Unstructured.

**Unstructured.io**: Select unstructured documents with multi-modal data, such as, text, PDFs and images.
