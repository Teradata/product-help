You can choose either Basic or Unstructured for your vector collection.

**Basic**: Select for fast and lightweight chunking. Content is split primarily by size and configurable rules.

This chunking method is best for simple text documents with predictable structure. For complex documents, you may have to split sentences which may result in format loss and affect the accurarcy of the results. You can fix this by using Unstructured.

**Note:** Basic chunking may not preserve semantic document structure.

**Unstructured**: Select Unstructured to have structure-aware chunking. This method preserves document elements, such as, headings, tables, lists, and sections.

This chunking method is recommended for PDFs, rich documents, and complex content. The higher quality chunks produced are useful for semantic search and RAG workloads.
