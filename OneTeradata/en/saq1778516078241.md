Configure the playground to interact with models, test prompts, and compare model behavior in a controlled chat environment.

**Prerequisite:** A virtual key is required. If you do not have one, contact your administrator.

1.  In the left navigation panel, select **Playground**.


1.  In **API key source**, enter your virtual key.


1.  In **Select model**, select a model associated with your virtual key.


1.  Verify that the **Endpoint type** is populated.


Your Playground is ready. In the **Chat** section, enter a prompt to evaluate how the model responds. To adjust parameters, see [Adjust chat parameters](wdn1778518249826.md).
